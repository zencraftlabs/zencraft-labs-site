General Information

Zencraft Labs ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data in relation to our Zendesk applications and our website.

Data Processing

Our applications, specifically the Field Validator, are designed with a "Privacy by Design" approach:

  • No External Storage: We do not store your Zendesk ticket data on our servers.
  • Local Execution: Validation logic typically runs within your Zendesk instance client-side.
  • No Tracking: We do not use intrusive tracking or analytics cookies within our functional apps.
